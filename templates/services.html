Services we Offer

At A.H. Bricks, we offer a comprehensive selection of high-quality construction materials to meet all your building needs. From foundations to finishes, we're your one-stop shop for a successful project. We understand budget constraints, and our team is happy to discuss cost-effective solutions to keep your project on track.

Bricks

Our Fly Ash Bricks are a revolutionary choice for eco-conscious builders. Made from a unique blend of fly ash, sand, and cement, these bricks boast exceptional hardness that actually improves over time – unlike traditional bricks. Free of harmful chemicals, they contribute to a healthier environment while delivering superior structural integrity for your project.

Chips

We offer high-quality aggregate stones, sourced from the renowned Gotidhara quarries, known for their exceptional roundness ideal for construction. Understanding project budget variations, we can also source stones from alternative crushers to meet your specific needs.

Sand

Sourced from the trusted sand ghats near Bisra, we offer a variety of sand particle sizes – small, medium, and large – to perfectly suit your construction needs. Whether you require fine sand for plastering or coarse sand for concrete, we provide high-quality options at competitive prices.

Cement

As a leading cement dealer with over 200 satisfied customers, we offer a wide selection of high-quality cements at competitive prices. We're authorized dealers for 2 major cement companies and trusted traders for over 5 additional brands. Our commitment to quality and affordability ensures you have the right materials to build strong and lasting structures.

Tmt Bar

We offer high-strength TMT (Thermo Mechanically Treated) bars for superior structural stability in your project. These bars are renowned for their enhanced ductility and weldability, ensuring a reliable and long-lasting foundation.

Water Tanker

Don't let water shortages slow down your construction project. We provide prompt and dependable water tanker services, ensuring a consistent supply of clean water for mixing concrete and other construction needs

Plywood and Furniture Items

We offer a comprehensive selection of high-quality plywood and furniture to complete your project. Choose from a variety of plywood options for dependable substructures, and explore our furniture selection to bring your vision to life. Visit our showroom or contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
